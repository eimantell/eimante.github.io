Who am I?

My name is Eimantė and I am a lithuanian girl. I am studying in LCC International University, majoring in Contemporary Communications. Most of the time people describe me as a positive, hyper-active and always smilling person. My biggest goal in life is to inspire people with everything that I do. That is why I try to use my social media and photography as a form of expression. I share my ideas, thoughts, interest and opinions. I hope that everyone in this world one day will find something worth living for.
